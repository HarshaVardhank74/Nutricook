About NutriCook

NutriCook is your intelligent assistant for healthier eating and meal planning.

Our Features:

  • Personalized Profiles: Securely store basic information like age and health conditions (optional) to help tailor advice.
  • Recipe Recommender: Get recipe suggestions based on specific nutritional goals (protein, fat, carbs, fiber) and ingredient preferences.
  • Recipe Generator: Feeling uninspired? Describe the type of meal you want (like "quick low-carb lunch" or "comfort food with pasta") and let our AI generate creative ideas with instructions.
  • AI Meal Checker: Snap a picture of your meal! Our AI analyzes the image to identify the food, estimate its nutritional content, and provide a quick assessment of whether it aligns with your stored health profile. (Note: This feature provides estimations and is not a substitute for professional dietary advice.)

Technology:

NutriCook utilizes cutting-edge AI and ML, to power its recommendation, generation, and analysis features. User data is handled securely, and passwords are encrypted.

Our Mission:

To make healthy eating more accessible, personalized, and informed through the power of technology.
